KG-CARES programme

KG-CARES Result Area 3 is an intervention of Kogi State government with support from World Bank to resuscitate business that were adversely affected by the Covid-19 pandemic.

KEDA Connect

EBIGO Foundation SME Awards: KEDA through the state government has been able to support the GYB/EBIGO awardees with One Million Naira (cash) to enhance their businesses .

KEDA NG- Cares

MAMF Global for Mushroom Farming: KEDA as the implementing partner for the mushroom farming led the team on an inspection for the proposed site for the Fungiculture Women Initiative. This is an ongoing partnership with Mbeng Adio Mushroom Farming (MAMF) global company in collaboration with the International Office, Delaware in USA.

Our Services

KEDA provides services such as creating business friendly environment through a comprehensive SME policy, improve access to new markets through facilitation of business friendly environment for intra, interstate and international border businesses, facilitate access to finance for MSMEs and provide access to information on multiple funding sources. Also, the agency supports MSMEs competitiveness and promote industry related innovation and competitiveness

Mission

To facilitate access to resource mobilisation for the growth of MSMEs across sectors.

Vision

To enhance capabilities of MSMEs for economic development and wealth creation.
